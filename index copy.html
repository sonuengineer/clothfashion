Our Heritage

Designed for Women, by Women

MAISON was founded with one belief: every woman deserves to feel powerful in what she wears. Our designs celebrate femininity, confidence, and individuality.

From our Milan atelier, each piece is crafted with the finest European fabrics — silk from Lake Como, wool from Scotland, lace from Calais — to create garments that feel as extraordinary as they look.

— Creative Director
